Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef Marie Dubois, Le Petit Four brings the authentic taste of French patisserie to your neighborhood. Using traditional recipes passed down through generations and the finest ingredients, we create pastries that transport you straight to the streets of Paris.

Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to prepare fresh bread, croissants, and pastries that delight our customers throughout the day.
