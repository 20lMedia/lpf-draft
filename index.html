Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef Marie Dubois, Le Petit Four brings the authentic taste of French patisserie to your neighborhood. Using traditional recipes passed down through generations and the finest ingredients, we create pastries that transport you straight to the streets of Paris.

Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to prepare fresh bread, croissants, and pastries that delight our customers throughout the day.

Our Chef

Marie Dubois

Executive Chef & Founder

Trained in Paris under renowned pastry masters, Marie brings 20 years of experience and a passion for traditional French techniques with modern twists. Her exceptional talent for creating delicate pastries and authentic French bread has earned her recognition throughout the culinary world.

After perfecting her craft in several prestigious Parisian bakeries, Marie moved abroad to share her love for French patisserie. In 2010, she founded Le Petit Four with the mission of bringing a true taste of France to every customer.
